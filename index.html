Pernambuco

Pernambuco, in northeastern Brazil, is known for its rich culture, history, and natural beauty. Its capital, Recife, features rivers and bridges, while Olinda offers colonial charm and vibrant Carnival celebrations. The state boasts stunning beaches like Porto de Galinhas and is a hub for traditional music and dance, such as frevo and maracatu, reflecting its Afro-Brazilian heritage. A captivating mix of history, culture, and scenery defines Pernambuco.
